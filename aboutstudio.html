Bears & Co by Svetlana Stakheeva

My studio, my favorite place for creativity is a part of my flat

I invented and renovated this space by myself. It's light, warmth and cosy place. There is permanent creative mess here but nonetheless all is on it's own place so I can take everything I need at any moment with closed eyes. Beside tools and materials there are my little collection of the author toys here, my friends' illustrations and collages, and a cork board (which I brought from Bali) with my notes, scetches and other lovely things. Each piece of this place was picked with a huge care and love by me. That's why it inspires me so much.

I placed photographs below for you to completely feel the atmosphere of the ABCbears studio. Enjoy!
